# M3 - Calendrier & Export

This scaffold prepares the calendar generation and PDF export feature.

## Optional dependency

- `weasyprint` is listed in `requirements-m3.txt`.
- Depending on your platform, WeasyPrint may need system libraries such as Cairo and Pango.

## Planned endpoints

- `GET /api/m3/calendar/html`
- `GET /api/m3/calendar/pdf`
